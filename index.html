Description

Elements Cubed is a fast-paced bullet hell game where you REK Computer Viruses! Run, Dive, and Dodge through perilous enemy fire and retaliate using an assortment of digital spells & abilities! Go it alone, or play it with a friend in couch CO-OP Mayhem!

History

I started working on this game in 2021 but then I abandoned it to work on a few different projects. Eventually I picked the project back up as an online multiplayer brawler game but it still didn't feel like something that would be fun to play. This led me to restructuring the entire game and turning it into a bullet hell roguelite instead. It's the type of game I love to play and make, so I'm really glad to be working on it!

Features

  • 17+ Abilities
  • 16+ Unique bosses
  • Life or death choices and alternate endings
  • Local co-op and PvP
  • A killer soundtrack!
